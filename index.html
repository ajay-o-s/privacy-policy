Welcome to AOS (Advanced Online Services), operated by Ajay O S. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and protect your personal information when you use our services, applications, and websites, including ajayos.in and its subdomains.

1. Policy Overview

This Privacy Policy governs all AOS services and explains:

  • What personal information we collect and why
  • How we use and protect your information
  • When and how we share information with third parties
  • Your rights and choices regarding your personal data
  • How to contact us about privacy concerns

Our Privacy Principles

  • Transparency: We clearly explain our data practices
  • Minimization: We collect only necessary information
  • Purpose Limitation: We use data only for stated purposes
  • Security: We implement robust protection measures
  • User Control: You have control over your data
  • Accountability: We take responsibility for data protection

This Privacy Policy applies to all AOS services, including:

Covered Services

  • Websites: ajayos.in and all subdomains

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Our Commitment: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We only share information in limited circumstances as outlined below.

When We Share Information

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes, subpoenas, or court orders
  • Respond to lawful requests from government authorities
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigate fraud or security incidents
  • Enforce our Terms of Service or other agreements

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

Data Processing Agreements

All service providers who process personal data on our behalf are bound by contractual obligations to:

  • Process data only for specified purposes
  • Maintain appropriate security measures
  • Not use data for their own purposes
  • Delete or return data when requested
  • Comply with applicable privacy laws

6. Data Storage and Security

Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit, AES encryption for data at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication for admin accounts
  • Firewalls: Network security and intrusion detection systems
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and software updates
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and incident response

Operational Security

  • Employee Training: Regular security awareness training
  • Background Checks: Screening for employees with data access
  • Incident Response: Established procedures for security breaches
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing

Data Storage Locations

Your data is primarily stored in secure data centers located in:

  • Primary: India (Mumbai, Bangalore regions)
  • Backup: Singapore, Ireland (for global redundancy)
  • CDN: Global content delivery network for performance
Data Breach Notification: In the unlikely event of a security incident affecting personal data, we will notify affected users within 72 hours and take immediate steps to contain and remedy the situation.

8. Your Privacy Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal data, which vary depending on your location and applicable laws.

Universal Rights

Access and Portability

  • View Your Data: Request a copy of all personal data we hold
  • Data Export: Download your data in machine-readable formats (JSON, CSV)
  • Account Dashboard: View and manage most data through your account settings

Correction and Updates

  • Profile Updates: Modify account information and preferences
  • Data Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Preference Management: Update communication and privacy preferences

Deletion and Restriction

  • Account Deletion: Permanently delete your account and associated data
  • Selective Deletion: Remove specific types of data
  • Processing Restriction: Limit how we process your data

Additional Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

If you're located in the EU, UK, or California, you have additional rights:

GDPR Rights (EU/UK)

  • Right to Object: Object to data processing based on legitimate interests
  • Automated Decision-Making: Opt out of automated profiling
  • Supervisory Authority: Lodge complaints with data protection authorities
  • Consent Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for specific processing activities

CCPA Rights (California)

  • Right to Know: Detailed information about data collection and use
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

How to Exercise Your Rights

Easy Access: Most privacy controls are available directly in your account dashboard. For additional requests, contact us using the information in the Contact section below.

To exercise your privacy rights:

  1. Account Dashboard: Use built-in privacy controls for most actions
  2. Email Request: Send detailed requests to privacy@ajayos.in
  3. Verification: We may need to verify your identity before processing requests
  4. Response Time: We'll respond within 30 days (EU) or 45 days (California)

You can control cookies through:

10. Children's Privacy

Age Restriction: AOS services are not intended for children under 10 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 10..

Child Safety Measures

  • Age Verification: We ask for age confirmation during account registration
  • Parental Consent: Users 10-17 require parental consent in some jurisdictions
  • Content Filtering: Age-appropriate content and community guidelines
  • Immediate Removal: If we discover a child's account, we delete it immediately

For Parents and Guardians

If you believe your child has created an account or shared personal information:

  • Contact us immediately at privacy@ajayos.in
  • Provide details about the account or information shared
  • We will investigate and take appropriate action within 24 hours
